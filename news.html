All Saints Church to be closed

All Saints Church, Wroxton will be closed from Monday 7th October, 2024 until further notice while organ restoration is carried out.

Wroxton Remembrance Service cancelled

It is with regret that, due to the ongoing organ restoration work and the upheaval the associated necessity of scaffolding erected in the church has created, this year's Remembrance Service cannot be held in our village church, and has therefore had to be canceled. Nevertheless, similar services are being held in Hornton, Horley, and Drayton churches at 10:50 am. Also, Balscote at 3pm. And at Alkerton Cenotaph on Monday 11th at 10:50am, which you are welcome to attend.

October Village Services to be in Wroxton Village Hall

The church services for Wroxton in October will be based at Wroxton Village Hall instead of All-Saints Church due to the closure.
