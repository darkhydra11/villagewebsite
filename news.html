Updates to local Recycling centers

Please note from 14th January you will need to pre-book a timed slot before you can visit the local Household Waste Recycling Centre, otherwise known as the tip! Bookings are available up to 2 weeks in advance, or 30 minutes prior, subject to availability. Please visit https://www.oxfordshire.gov.uk/waste-and-recycling/book-recycling-centre-visit to book, you will be asked to choose your local household waste recycling centre, enter the type of waste you are bringing, select your date and time and then enter your details. You will then be sent a confirmation email with all the relevant details, the email will explain what to do if you have any chargeable waste and your weekly free allowance. Please note, when you visit you must take proof of your address, a council tax or utility bill and you MUST arrive in the vehicle specified in your booking form.

If you cannot book online, you can book by calling the Customer Service Centre on 01865 519800, phone lines are open from 8.45am-5pm Monday to Thursday and 8.45am-4pm on Fridays.
