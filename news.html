Wroxton & Balscote Parish Council offers Invitation to Tender for Grass Cutting Contract from 2025-2027

Wroxton & Balscote Parish Council invites tenders for a 3 year grass cutting contract in the villages of Wroxton & Balscote. Please apply to the Parish Clerk for more information via email to Gail Williams by clicking here. Please provide your name, address and telephone number. The closing date for returning tenders to the Parish Clerk is 30th January 2025.
