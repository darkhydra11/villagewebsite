Latest News

All Saints Church re-opens

All Saints Church, Wroxton has now re-opened, although the organ restoration is still ongoing. Services in the church will resume from Sunday 8th December, 2024.

There will also be cleaning of the church on Friday 6th December, 2024 from 10:30am, in which you are more than welcome to assist with.

Road in Wroxton Heath set to close for civils work

The main road in Wroxton Road will be closing between Monday 16th December up to and including Friday 20th December for civil works.
