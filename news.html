All Saints Church re-opens

All Saints Church, Wroxton has now re-opened in relation to the organ restoration. Services in the church will resume from Sunday 8th December, 2024.

There will also be cleaning of the church on Friday 6th December, 2024 from 10:30am, in which you are more than welcome to assist with.

Wroxton Remembrance Service Cancelled

It is with regret that this year's Remembrance Service cannot be held in our village church, and has therefore had to be canceled.
