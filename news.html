Wroxton Resident completes London Marathon!!

Lauren Wade (from Church Street) completed the London Marathon last Sunday (27th). Thank you to everyone for the good luck wishes, support & donations, they really were greatly appreciated. Her time was 5hrs 45mins but that included 25 mins of stops for blister treatment - and it was so hot for most of the day!

Robin Russell

Robin Russell, who was the former landlord of the North Arms pub for many years, sadly passed away recently. His funeral has been arranged to take place at Banbury Crematorium on Thursday 8th May, 2025 at 11am.

Wroxton & Balscote Parish Council offers Invitation to Tender for Grass Cutting Contract from 2025-2027

Wroxton & Balscote Parish Council invites tenders for a 3 year grass cutting contract in the villages of Wroxton & Balscote. Please apply to the Parish Clerk for more information via email to Gail Williams. Please provide your name, address and telephone number. The closing date for returning tenders to the Parish Clerk is 30th January 2025.
