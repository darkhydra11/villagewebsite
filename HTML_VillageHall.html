Welcome to Wroxton Village Hall! Our charming venue offers an unparalleled event experience, with food handling facilities, a small stage, and plenty of tables and chairs. Our spacious parking lot is an added convenience for guests, and our friendly committee is on hand to make your event experience unforgettable. If you are looking for a great location for your next event, we encourage you to contact us! Our dedicated team is here to assist you in selecting the perfect date and planning your event at Wroxton Village Hall. Call Sam Mitchell, our bookings manager, at 01295 730 065 to make a booking or schedule a viewing!
