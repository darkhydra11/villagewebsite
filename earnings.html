This year the Fete Profit is being split 3 ways:

  • £2,085 to be donated to Wroxton Community Fund
  • £2,085 to be donated to All Saints Church, Wroxton for the church clock repair fund
  • £2,085 to be allocated as a Charitable Donation:
    • £500 will be used to purchase boxing equipment for the Spit’n Sawdust Boxing & Fitness Club in Banbury, run by Dave Earle MBE. Dave is an inspirational trainer who has done a tremendous amount of work to support young people facing difficult times. Through the boxing club, he helps to inspire and motivate participants, and has played a significant role in turning around many lives. A formal handover date for the equipment will be announced in due course.
    • The remaining balance will be used to support the Wroxton Duck Fund account, including duck hut repairs, feeding equipment, ongoing and rising food costs, as well as purchasing some more ducks as all the very attractive Silver Appleyard’s have mysteriously disappeared.
